Domain Management

Registrars & Name Servers

  • Transferring a domain from one registrar to another
    • Go to the current registrar and "Unlock" the domain transfer flag.
    • Wait for a few hours for this unlock flag to propagate, and then check for transfer unlock with the new registrar.
    • When the flag propagates, go to the old registrar, and generate an "Auth Code", and enter it in the new registrar to authorize the transfer.
    • The old registrar will send you an email/reach out to you to confirm the domain transfer. In my case, I got an email with a link to a confirmation form as shown here.

      After clicking "Approve", the domain will be released from the old registrar and transfered to the new one.
